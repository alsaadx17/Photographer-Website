LIFE IN THE HAOR
IS A PARADOX.

The sun bears down with a merciless glare, the air thick with thirst. There are no roads—only water, endless and shifting.

Boats are not a choice, but a necessity. Life here is raw, stripped to its bones.

Yet within the hardship, there's something else—something haunting.

The haor wears its beauty like a storm: fierce, unpredictable, and breathtaking. It doesn't ask to be admired; it dares you to understand it.

NETROKONA, MYMENSINGH, BANGLADESH. 2023
